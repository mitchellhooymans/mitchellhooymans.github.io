Research Projects

Exploring the dynamics of the universe through simulation and theory.

Current PhD Project

Black Hole Dynamics in Star Clusters

Investigating how stellar-mass black holes interact within dense globular clusters. Using N-body simulations, we aim to understand the formation of binary black holes that eventually merge and emit gravitational waves.

  • Developing high-precision N-body codes.
  • Analyzing merger rates for LIGO/Virgo comparisons.
  • Studying dynamical friction and mass segregation effects.
View Publications
Computational Method

Parallel N-Body Algorithms

Optimizing gravitational force calculations using GPU acceleration and improved tree-code algorithms. This research focuses on scaling simulations to millions of particles while maintaining energy conservation.

Tools: Python, C++, CUDA, MPI
